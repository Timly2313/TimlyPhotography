The Photographer

Timly captures

what others miss

Born and raised in Johannesburg, Timly discovered photography at 16 through a borrowed film camera and a burning need to preserve the beauty hidden in ordinary moments. Two decades later, that instinct has become a life's work.

Shooting across the Cape Winelands, Drakensberg highlands, and internationally, Timly brings a fashion editorial sensibility to deeply personal work creating images that feel as timeless as the stories they hold.

The Philosophy
01

THE LIGHT

We chase natural light — the golden hour warmth before sunset, the soft diffusion through white curtains at dawn, the dramatic rim light of a storm breaking.

02

THE MOMENT

Unposed, honest, alive. We believe the best images happen in the spaces between directions — a glance shared, a laugh that couldn't be staged.

03

THE STORY

Every image tells a chapter of something larger. We curate galleries that read like a love letter — a narrative arc from beginning to the frame that stopped time.
